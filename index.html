Welcome to my github pages website. You might say the design is unique... and though I could have opted for a pre-fab UI/UX with far superior appeal, this format is more befitting to the collage of projects and methods that I have worked on.
Here you will find various notes, musings, and the occasional rambling on various science-related topics that interest me (both personal and professional).
Many posts are directly related to research that I have conducted in the past or that I am actively working on, while others are simply hobbies of mine, research interests, or freebies and tutorials that I am releasing in hopes that they might help others.

For those interested; here's a brief summary of my professional experience:
>10 years in NMR Facility Operations, specializing in Biomolecular NMR Spectroscopy
>5 years as a technical and scientific leader in industrial R&D from biomanufacturing to analytical method development
Rapid prototyping to integrate software and automation tools into analytical workflows.
